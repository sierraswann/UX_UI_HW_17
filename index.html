Hey Ya'll! I'm Sierra; an inquisitive mind, creative soul, and sweet Georgia peach. As a Theater major in college, I have utilized my listening, collaboration, and creative skills to help me on my journey as a UX/UI designer. My goal is to continue to create designs that are not only beautiful, but also solve problems and make technology accessible to everyone.
